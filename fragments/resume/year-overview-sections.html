One of the best image optimization tools on the markup.

Imager is a tool for automated image compression, and can competitively optimize very noisy, high resolution images into rather “tiny” files. It's pretty easy too.

It uses ML based metrics in a trial ’n error sorta manner. Everything is free and open source, with absolutely no SAAS lock-ins or vendors.
